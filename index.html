About Petit Ilot

À Propos de Petit Ilot

Why Petit Ilot?

The idea for Petit Ilot emerged from years of working with children and observing a gap in Montreal's play landscape. While outdoor playgrounds abound in summer, families needed a year-round solution that goes beyond simple entertainment to truly support child development.

We noticed that many existing indoor play spaces focus primarily on physical exhaustion rather than holistic development. Parents often felt like spectators rather than participants in their children's play experiences. This disconnect inspired us to reimagine what an indoor play space could be.

Our Design Process

Petit Ilot wasn't designed by architects alone, but through extensive research into child development theory and practical experience with hundreds of children. Our design process involved:

  • Developmental Research: Studying how different play types support cognitive, physical, social and emotional growth
  • Safety Innovation: Implementing standards that exceed Canadian and European requirements
  • Inclusive Design: Ensuring accessibility for children with varying physical and cognitive abilities
  • Family Integration: Creating equipment and spaces that encourage active parent participation
  • Sensory Consideration: Balancing stimulation and calm to support all sensory processing needs

Our Expertise

The Petit Ilot team brings together complementary expertise that uniquely positions us to create this innovative space:

  • Sports Science Background: Deep understanding of motor skill development, physical literacy, and safe equipment design
  • Early Childhood Education Expertise: Specialized knowledge in developmental milestones, inclusive programming, and supporting children with diverse needs
  • 15+ Years Combined Experience: Hands-on work with children from infancy through school age in various educational and recreational settings
  • Inclusive Approach: Extensive experience supporting children with specific needs, ensuring our space welcomes all families

This combination of theoretical knowledge and practical experience drives every decision we make, from equipment selection to program design.

Our Vision

We envision Petit Ilot as more than a play space – it's a community hub where:

  • Children develop confidence through age-appropriate challenges
  • Parents actively engage in their child's play rather than watching from sidelines
  • Families create lasting memories through shared experiences
  • Every child, regardless of ability, finds joy and growth opportunities
  • Montreal families have a premium, safe space available year-round

Pourquoi Petit Ilot ?

L'idée de Petit Ilot a émergé après des années de travail auprès d'enfants et l'observation d'une lacune dans le paysage ludique montréalais. Bien que les terrains de jeux extérieurs abondent l'été, les familles avaient besoin d'une solution année-longue qui va au-delà du simple divertissement pour vraiment soutenir le développement de l'enfant.

Nous avons remarqué que beaucoup d'espaces de jeux intérieurs existants se concentrent principalement sur l'épuisement physique plutôt que sur le développement holistique. Les parents se sentaient souvent comme des spectateurs plutôt que des participants dans les expériences de jeu de leurs enfants. Cette déconnexion nous a inspirés à réimaginer ce qu'un espace de jeux intérieur pourrait être.

Notre Processus de Conception

Petit Ilot n'a pas été conçu uniquement par des architectes, mais à travers une recherche approfondie sur la théorie du développement de l'enfant et une expérience pratique avec des centaines d'enfants. Notre processus de conception impliquait :

  • Recherche Développementale : Étude de la façon dont différents types de jeu soutiennent la croissance cognitive, physique, sociale et émotionnelle
  • Innovation Sécuritaire : Mise en œuvre de normes qui dépassent les exigences canadiennes et européennes
  • Conception Inclusive : Assurer l'accessibilité pour les enfants avec des capacités physiques et cognitives variées
  • Intégration Familiale : Créer des équipements et espaces qui encouragent la participation parentale active
  • Considération Sensorielle : Équilibrer stimulation et calme pour soutenir tous les besoins de traitement sensoriel

Notre Expertise

L'équipe Petit Ilot réunit une expertise complémentaire qui nous positionne uniquement pour créer cet espace innovant :

  • Formation en Sciences du Sport : Compréhension approfondie du développement des habiletés motrices, de la littératie physique et de la conception d'équipements sécuritaires
  • Expertise en Éducation de la Petite Enfance : Connaissances spécialisées des étapes développementales, programmation inclusive et soutien aux enfants avec besoins diversifiés
  • Plus de 15 Ans d'Expérience Combinée : Travail pratique avec des enfants de la petite enfance à l'âge scolaire dans divers contextes éducatifs et récréatifs
  • Approche Inclusive : Expérience extensive dans le soutien d'enfants avec besoins spécifiques, assurant que notre espace accueille toutes les familles

Cette combinaison de connaissances théoriques et d'expérience pratique guide chaque décision que nous prenons, de la sélection d'équipements à la conception de programmes.

Notre Vision

Nous envisageons Petit Ilot comme plus qu'un espace de jeux – c'est un hub communautaire où :

  • Les enfants développent leur confiance à travers des défis adaptés à leur âge
  • Les parents participent activement au jeu de leur enfant plutôt que d'observer depuis les gradins
  • Les familles créent des souvenirs durables à travers des expériences partagées
  • Chaque enfant, peu importe ses capacités, trouve de la joie et des opportunités de croissance
  • Les familles montréalaises ont un espace premium et sécuritaire disponible à l'année

Motor Skills Zone: Physical Development in Freedom

Zone Motrice : Le Développement Physique en Liberté

An Adaptive and Safe Approach

Our motor skills zone adapts to the specific needs of each age group through a reconfiguration system between sessions. The equipment is designed to support adult weight, encouraging parents to actively participate in their children's motor discoveries.

High-Quality Equipment

Gymnastics Mat

Professional surface allowing rolls, jumps and movements in complete safety, compliant with CSA Z614 standards.

Secure Foam Pit

Soft landing zone for jumps and acrobatics, encouraging calculated risk-taking essential for building self-confidence.

Modular Climbing Structures

Evolutionary courses adapted according to the age of the session, developing strength, coordination and spatial problem solving.

Slides and Play Structures

Fun elements stimulating balance, proprioception and the joy of movement.

Developmental Benefits

  • Gross Motor Skills: Development of strength, balance, coordination
  • Body Confidence: Knowledge and acceptance of physical limits
  • Calculated Risk-Taking: Learning to assess challenges
  • Cooperation: Sharing space and mutual help between children

Une Approche Évolutive et Sécuritaire

Finding the Perfect Location

Trouver l'Emplacement Parfait

The search for our ideal location is well underway, and we're excited to share what we're looking for and why location is so crucial to the Petit Ilot experience.

Our Location Criteria

We're seeking a special space that meets our high standards:

  • Size: Minimum 3,500 square feet to accommodate our three distinct play zones
  • Ceiling Height: At least 12 feet to safely install our climbing structures and play equipment
  • Accessibility: Ground floor with excellent wheelchair and stroller access
  • Parking: Ample parking for families with young children
  • Safety: Secure location with controlled access and visibility

Target Neighborhoods

We're focusing our search on family-friendly neighborhoods, particularly Villeray and surrounding areas. These communities are known for their strong family presence, walkability, and access to public transportation - all important factors for our families.

Current Progress

We've already visited several promising locations and are working closely with commercial real estate agents who understand our unique requirements. Each potential space is being evaluated not just for its physical attributes, but for how well it will serve our community of families.

Equipment Selection & Safety Standards

Sélection d'Équipements & Normes de Sécurité

Safety is our top priority at Petit Ilot, and we're taking no shortcuts when it comes to selecting equipment that will provide years of safe, developmental play for Montreal families.

Safety Standards We Follow

All our equipment will meet or exceed:

  • Canadian Standards (CSA Z614): Playground equipment and surfacing requirements
  • European Standards (EN 1176): Additional safety requirements for playground equipment
  • Health Canada Guidelines: For child-safe materials and finishes
  • Fire Safety Codes: Meeting all municipal requirements

Equipment Selection Process

We're working exclusively with certified suppliers who specialize in commercial-grade play equipment. Each piece is being selected not just for fun, but for its developmental benefits and ability to support children of varying abilities and needs.

Special Features

What sets our equipment apart:

  • Adult-Weight Capacity: All equipment designed to support parent participation
  • Sensory Integration: Textures, sounds, and visual elements that support sensory development
  • Adaptive Design: Equipment accessible to children with varying physical abilities
  • Easy Maintenance: Antimicrobial surfaces and easy-to-clean materials
